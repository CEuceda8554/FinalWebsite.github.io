Honduras' Places

Honduras has many dazzling places to visit with its natural beauty and diverse wildlife. Nature lovers can explore many beaches, lakes, jungles, and tropical rainforests.

Bay Islands are reigned by thriving coral reefs, part of the worldâ€™s second biggest barrier reef, luring in many divers, snorkelers, and travelers seeking a relaxing tropical vibe.

Copan Ruins Archeological Site is the most studied Maya city in the world, dating back nearly 2,000 years.

The Museum of Mayan Sculpture is an essential stop on any visit to the ruins. It displays a series of original pieces of sculpture, stelae, and altars recovered from the site.

La Tigra National Park is one of the most beautiful places to visit in Honduras. This park preserves a lush cloud forest that is home to ocelots, pumas, and monkeys.
